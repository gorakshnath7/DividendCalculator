    How to use the Dividend Calculator

    A dividend calculator is a valuable tool for investors to estimate potential income from a stock investment based on dividends. These calculators typically require input values such as the dividend per share and the share price to provide the dividend yield, which represents the proportion of dividends paid per share relative to the share price. Investors can use dividend calculators to assess the income they might earn from their investments over a specified period.
